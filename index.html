Our story

Why Harvest & Co.

We started Harvest & Co. with one simple idea: produce should travel from soil to your kitchen in hours, not weeks. We work directly with small local farms, skip unnecessary storage, and deliver everything the same day it's picked — so what lands on your table actually tastes like it grew somewhere real.
